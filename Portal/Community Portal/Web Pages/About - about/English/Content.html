How can it help me?

The network will help you develop digital skills and make the most of technology available to you and your organisation, from data analytics and web design to finding new ways to reach and engage audiences and developing sustainable business models.
You'll be able to access support through training, events, one to one surgeries, online resources and more, all with the help of our nine expert Tech Champions. We’ll be developing partnerships and identifying opportunities with the tech sector, sharing best practice and providing signposting and practical on-the-ground support.
